The Next Evolution Of Social Media Marketing

PROBLEMS IN THE MARKETING INDUSTRY

Previous marketing strategies simply aren’t as effective as they once were:

Technology and Social Media have changed the world of investing. There’s a growing competitive climate, and standing out from others has become significantly harder.

Few companies take advantage of the new avenues to tell their story

It’s hard to stand out when hundreds of other companies are putting out news every day

There’s often a new exciting player with a new story, keeping an individual's attention is hard using existing channels

While investors are adapting to new ways of communication using social media, companies are often left behind using the same channels they’ve been using for the past 10 years.

Social media has given investors the ability to stay up to date with a company’s day to day operations, but investors are often left wanting more than a company can provide using the standard weekly/biweekly update approach.

Investors and customers will choose a company that’s more socially engaging with a story to follow rather than a company with better financials and a dormant story.

The marketing and promotional industry is filled with pump and dumps. Traditional firms only offer one core service, selling newsletters and pushing out emails that simply don’t yield the results they once did.

With most firms sending 20 emails a week to the same database, the value of an email list can be lost.

News flow is a common issue for companies. No news traditionally means no updates for shareholders. Without news investors are left in the dark, which leads to uncertainty and doubt in a company’s ability and plan. Ultimately, a lack of communication makes it increasingly likely investors will jump ship!

    THE CAMPAIGN

    CREATING VIDEO CONTENT

    Creating weekly videos is a great way to not only stand apart from the rest, it’s a great way to engage with current investors in ways other than press releases and newsletters. Videos capture personality, humour, and excitement more than written medians, they also allow a more in depth look at recent news releases from the company's perspective. Videos act as a great touch point with investors when there isn’t any significant news.

    GROUP CHATS

    Group chats and crowd opinions are becoming a social norm. Having a presence on medians like Reddit, Facebook, Investorshub, etc is extremely important. These websites combine for over 80M investors in North America alone and are great medians to find new investors for companies. Having the ability to spread videos and company updates across these channels guarantees you’ll stand out more than companies who aren’t.

    LINKEDIN

    Linkedin has the most opportunity for a social network, being able to target individuals by past investments they made, their job title, and even where they live. We’re able to keep a busy pipeline of potential investors who are eager to learn about the company and have shown interest in the industry before!
